FAQ

Common questions

How often is the directory updated?

Weekly with new businesses from Google Places, and daily with self-submitted listings from business owners.

Are listings verified?

Listings marked "Verified" come directly from Google Places and include real business data. Self-submitted listings are reviewed within 48 hours before going live.

How much does it cost to list my business?

Completely free. No paid tiers, no commission, no hidden costs. We're funded by Google AdSense advertising.

Can I update my listing?

Yes — use the "List Your Business" form and mention you'd like to update an existing listing. Include your business name and the changes needed.

How do I find a mobile groomer near me?

Use the "Mobile Grooming" filter chip on the homepage directory, or search for your suburb name in the search bar.
