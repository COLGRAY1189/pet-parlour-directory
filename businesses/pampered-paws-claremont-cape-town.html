I feel that it was wrong since my dog's appointment was for 09h30 already and then they ended up rushing the whole process and then chasing me on just before closing time. The front desk staff member apologised when I asked about the 4 hour waiting time and he said they were understaffed which I understand and I sympathise with, BUT I am less tolerant of the lack of care and gentleness they displayed during my dog's groom and the fact that they hurt her badly this time. I've always supported pampered paws but I am beyond disappointed and let down by this experience and I would consider taking my dog to alternative parlor where she is in better hands.
